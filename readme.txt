== Description ==

1-Visit the checkout page on the website where you are making a purchase using WooCommerce.

2-On the checkout page, you will see a list of products you have added to your cart.

3-Next to each product, you will find a quantity input field.

4-To change the quantity of a product, locate the quantity input field corresponding to that product.

5-Click inside the quantity input field and enter the desired quantity for that product. You can either type the number directly or use the up and down arrows to increase or decrease the quantity.

6-Once you have entered the desired quantity, the total price for that product will update automatically.

7-Repeat the process for any other products in your cart that you wish to change the quantity for.

8-After adjusting the quantities as desired, proceed with the checkout process by providing the required information, such as shipping address and payment details.

By following these steps, you can easily change the quantity of products on the WooCommerce checkout page to ensure you have the desired quantities before completing your purchase.
